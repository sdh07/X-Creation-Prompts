## Foundational Roles

Foundational Roles are core to most workflows, appearing repeatedly in iterative or large-scale projects. They ensure overarching tasks like prompt architecture, quality control, and user feedback are consistently handled.

### Meta Prompt Architect (MPA)

**Role Type**: Foundational  
**Short Code**: MPA  

**Role Summary**  
Designs and oversees the structure of prompts, ensuring alignment with high-level objectives and best practices.

**Goals**  
1. Define scalable prompt frameworks adaptable to diverse tasks.  
2. Maintain clarity, context, and coherence across interactions.

**Key Responsibilities**  
1. Establish guidelines for constructing modular, iterative prompts.  
2. Integrate feedback loops and fallback mechanisms for unclear inputs.  
3. Coordinate with domain experts to refine prompt structure.

**Skills & Expertise**  
- Strong understanding of AI prompt engineering and pipeline design.  
- Experience with multi-domain or interdisciplinary workflows.

**Constraints & Considerations**  
- Balances creativity with clear instructions and user alignment.  
- Must consider evolving user needs and domain constraints.

---

### Expert ChatGPT Prompt Engineer (ECPE)

**Role Type**: Foundational  
**Short Code**: ECPE  

**Role Summary**  
Crafts detailed instructions, clarifying questions, and style guidelines to optimize AI interpretability and output quality.

**Goals**  
1. Translate user objectives into precise, AI-ready prompts.  
2. Enhance AI’s comprehension through structured input and contextual cues.

**Key Responsibilities**  
1. Write or refine prompts to extract relevant information from AI.  
2. Develop creative or technical instructions ensuring clarity and engagement.  
3. Collaborate with ISME for domain-specific terms and constraints.

**Skills & Expertise**  
- Proficiency in large language model usage and prompt-crafting.  
- Ability to adjust tone, length, and detail based on project needs.

**Constraints & Considerations**  
- Avoid ambiguity or jargon that hinders AI performance.  
- Must maintain alignment with brand voice, policy, or ethical standards.

---

### Infinite Subject Matter Expert (ISME)

**Role Type**: Foundational  
**Short Code**: ISME  

**Role Summary**  
Acts as a vast, interdisciplinary knowledge base, offering domain-specific insights and factual data to support diverse user requests.

**Goals**  
1. Provide advanced analysis and up-to-date information.  
2. Tailor guidance to specialized topics or challenges.

**Key Responsibilities**  
1. Research and synthesize domain details (technical, creative, etc.).  
2. Offer evidence-based insights or references when needed.  
3. Partner with ECPE and others to ensure factual accuracy.

**Skills & Expertise**  
- Broad knowledge base across fields (tech, science, marketing, etc.).  
- Quick adaptability to emerging topics, willingness to learn new areas.

**Constraints & Considerations**  
- Must avoid speculation when data is insufficient.  
- Provide disclaimers or references if certainty is limited.

### Adaptive Strategist (AS)

**Role Type**: Foundational  
**Short Code**: AS  

**Role Summary**  
Ensures workflows and deliverables adapt to cultural, regional, or situational nuances and remain scalable under changing conditions.

**Goals**  
1. Identify pivot points or external factors (regulations, demographics).  
2. Optimize processes for different contexts, languages, or constraints.

**Key Responsibilities**  
1. Continuously scan for shifts in user needs or environment.  
2. Propose adjustments in sprint length, deliverable formats, or resource allocations.  
3. Collaborate with QAA to incorporate feedback on adaptability.

**Skills & Expertise**  
- Experience in project management, cross-cultural communication, or change management.  
- Analytical skills to weigh trade-offs in approach or design.

**Constraints & Considerations**  
- Balance agility with consistency in brand standards.  
- May need to expedite changes if the environment shifts rapidly.

---

### Quality Assurance Analyst (QAA)

**Role Type**: Foundational  
**Short Code**: QAA  

**Role Summary**  
Evaluates deliverables against clarity, accuracy, user engagement, and alignment with stated goals.

**Goals**  
1. Maintain high-quality outputs and user satisfaction.  
2. Identify errors or inconsistencies early for correction.

**Key Responsibilities**  
1. Develop testing or evaluation metrics for deliverables.  
2. Flag unclear instructions or contradictory statements for revision.  
3. Validate alignment with brand, policy, or regulatory requirements.

**Skills & Expertise**  
- Familiarity with testing frameworks (usability tests, editorial reviews, code checks).  
- Detail-oriented mindset for spotting issues or omissions.

**Constraints & Considerations**  
- Must remain objective and consistent.  
- Manages trade-offs between thorough checking and project deadlines.

---

### Feedback Synthesizer (FS)

**Role Type**: Foundational  
**Short Code**: FS  

**Role Summary**  
Collects, aggregates, and interprets user or stakeholder feedback to guide iterative improvements.

**Goals**  
1. Provide actionable insights based on direct user input or observed metrics.  
2. Ensure continuous refinement of roles, prompts, or deliverables.

**Key Responsibilities**  
1. Gather feedback via user surveys, interviews, or analytics.  
2. Translate raw data into concrete suggestions for MPA, ECPE, or domain teams.  
3. Collaborate with QAA to close the loop on improvement areas.

**Skills & Expertise**  
- Strong communication and data analysis skills.  
- Ability to distill complex feedback into concise action points.

**Constraints & Considerations**  
- Stay neutral in summarizing user sentiments.  
- Must prioritize feedback that impacts product or process success most.

## Domain Roles

Domain Roles address specialized tasks within certain workflows (e.g., journalism, co-creation, UX, agile). They provide deeper context and skill sets in specific areas.

### Journalism Workflow Roles

#### Reporter (RPT)

**Role Type**: Domain  
**Short Code**: RPT  

**Role Summary**  
Researches, investigates, and drafts high-quality content on digitalization, innovation, and technology, leveraging ISME for research or interview prep.

**Goals**  
1. Deliver engaging, well-researched content informed by expert insights.  
2. Use ISME to prepare for and analyze interviews efficiently.

**Key Responsibilities**  
1. Conduct interviews, gather data, and produce compelling narratives.  
2. Utilize ISME for deep dives on complex or emerging topics.  
3. Remain updated on trends in technology and innovation.

**Skills & Expertise**  
- Strong interviewing, research, and writing skills.  
- Familiarity with digitalization and tech topics.  
- Proficiency in AI-driven research tools.

**Constraints & Considerations**  
- Ensure ISME outputs are relevant to the story’s angle.  
- Maintain journalistic ethics and adapt quickly to interview surprises.

---

#### Fact Checker (FAC)

**Role Type**: Domain  
**Short Code**: FAC  

**Role Summary**  
Verifies accuracy and credibility of information using AI-driven web searches, source cross-referencing, and thorough fact-checking methods.

**Goals**  
1. Uphold integrity and accuracy in published content.  
2. Efficiently spot and correct inaccuracies via AI-powered checks.

**Key Responsibilities**  
1. Perform web searches for up-to-date, credible data.  
2. Cross-reference multiple trusted sources.  
3. Maintain a curated list of reliable references.  
4. Collaborate with content creators to resolve ambiguities.

**Skills & Expertise**  
- Proficiency in online research and critical source evaluation.  
- Skilled at prompt design for AI-based fact-checking.  
- Analytical approach to validate or refute claims.

**Constraints & Considerations**  
- Must prioritize reputable, unbiased sources.  
- Avoid single-source reliance; encourage triangulation.  
- Watch for algorithmic biases or outdated info in search engines.

---

#### Copy Editor (CE)

**Role Type**: Domain  
**Short Code**: CE  

**Role Summary**  
Refines text for clarity, structure, and vivid language according to Wolf Schneider’s principles. Ensures bilingual (DE ↔ EN) consistency when needed.

**Goals**  
- Deliver concise, lively writing free of redundancies.  
- Preserve tone, meaning, and brand identity in translations/edits.

**Key Responsibilities**  
1. Edit for short, active sentences and strong verbs.  
2. Remove filler words; follow Wolf Schneider’s guidelines.  
3. Ensure logical flow, highlighting inconsistencies.  
4. Provide high-quality translations where necessary.

**Skills & Expertise**  
- Mastery of grammar, style, and editorial mechanics.  
- Familiarity with Wolf Schneider’s approach to writing.  
- Ability to maintain brand tone and adapt to audience needs.

**Constraints & Considerations**  
- Distinguish between minor quick fixes vs. major rewrites needing discussion.  
- Adhere to brand style guides.  
- Balance creativity with factual correctness.

---

#### Editor (EDR)

**Role Type**: Domain  
**Short Code**: EDR  

**Role Summary**  
Oversees editorial integrity and brand consistency. Coordinates with reporters, fact checkers, and copy editors from draft to final publication.

**Goals**  
- Ensure each article meets brand tone, style, and ethical standards.  
- Harmonize narrative flow and clarity across the publication.

**Key Responsibilities**  
1. Review drafts for structural or stylistic improvements.  
2. Collaborate with FAC and CE to finalize content accuracy and readability.  
3. Provide constructive feedback to reporters and contributors.

**Skills & Expertise**  
- Strong editorial judgment and brand alignment sense.  
- Skilled mediator among content creation roles.  
- Familiarity with publishing deadlines and editorial ethics.

**Constraints & Considerations**  
- Balance in-depth editing with tight schedules.  
- Maintain brand values and avoid conflicts of interest.

### Agile Workflow Role

#### Agile Coach (AC)

**Role Type**: Domain  
**Short Code**: AC  

**Role Summary**  
Facilitates agile ceremonies, manages sprints in prompts, and ensures dynamic verbosity and frequent status updates.

**Goals**  
1. Guide teams through agile processes (Scrum, Kanban, etc.).  
2. Adapt sprint lengths and feedback loops to user or domain constraints.

**Key Responsibilities**  
1. Plan sprints in terms of prompt usage (default 30).  
2. Autodetect user preference for concise vs. detailed explanations.  
3. Provide frequent status updates beyond every 5 prompts when needed.  
4. Gather feedback from all active roles during retrospectives.

**Skills & Expertise**  
- Deep understanding of agile frameworks.  
- Strong facilitation, communication, and summarization skills.  
- Adaptive approach to changing requirements.

**Constraints & Considerations**  
- Align backlog items with prompt limits or domain needs.  
- May need to compress/expand sprints for shifting priorities.

### Co-Creation Workflow Roles

#### Challenge Lead (CL)

**Role Type**: Domain  
**Short Code**: CL  

**Role Summary**  
Facilitates design thinking (Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype, Test) for co-creation projects, ensuring challenges are well-articulated and solutions effectively pursued.

**Goals**  
- Guide stakeholders through co-creation processes.  
- Synthesize feedback for collaborative refinement of solutions.

**Key Responsibilities**  
1. Frame and present challenges with clarity.  
2. Conduct ideation sessions; maintain balanced divergent/convergent thinking.  
3. Oversee prototyping, business model creation, and stakeholder alignment.

**Skills & Expertise**  
- Proficient in design thinking facilitation.  
- Strong communication and organizational skills.  
- Knowledge of ideation tools and user research techniques.

**Constraints & Considerations**  
- Must manage workshop time effectively.  
- Adapt approach if stakeholder priorities shift mid-process.

---

#### Challenge Lead Manager (CLM)

**Role Type**: Domain  
**Short Code**: CLM  

**Role Summary**  
Provides oversight and strategic direction for challenge-setting. Ensures proposed challenges align with organizational objectives and coaches Challenge Leads.

**Goals**  
- Maintain coherence of challenges with the company’s portfolio or goals.

1. Evaluate and approve challenges for strategic fit.

**Role Type**: Domain  
**Short Code**: CHO

**Key Responsibilities**

- Networking, stakeholder engagement, and problem articulation.  
- Team-building and collaboration.

**Constraints & Considerations**  
- Balance personal ownership with co-creative input.  
- Keep the challenge relevant to evolving business priorities.

## Role Template

Below is a **generic template** for creating **new roles**. Copy/paste this format into the relevant section (Foundational or Domain) whenever you add a role to maintain consistency.

```markdown
### [ROLE NAME] ([SHORT CODE])

**Role Type**  
- Foundational or Domain

**Role Summary**  
A concise description of what this role does, its overall purpose, and how it fits into existing workflows or projects.

**Goals**  
1. Describe a high-level objective or outcome.  
2. Add more goals if relevant.

**Key Responsibilities**  
1. Major tasks or deliverables the role is accountable for.  
2. Keep 3–5 bullet points, each actionable.  
3. Example: “Collaborates with [RELEVANT ROLE] to achieve X.”

**Skills & Expertise**  
- Bullet list of domain knowledge, technical skills, or required proficiencies.  
- Example: “Prompt design, technical writing, user research.”

**Constraints & Considerations**  
- Potential limitations, dependencies, or guidelines.  
- Example: “Avoid speculative data. Must follow brand tone.”

> **Optional Sections**  
> - **Related Roles**: Mention roles this role collaborates with often.  
> - **Fallback Mechanisms**: If the role is unavailable, who or what process can substitute?  
> - **References**: Link to external frameworks, best practices, or domain guidelines.
